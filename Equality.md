- Educational attainment varies by class

- Persistent disparities documented
- Access influenced by background

- Higher social mobility levels
- Progressive taxation implemented
- Strong safety nets
- Universal services provided
- Results show reduced inequality

- Debate involves finding balance
- Maximize freedom and efficiency
- Ensure fairness and justice
- Opportunity emphasizes level playing field
- Individual merit can thrive
- Outcome stresses correcting advantages
- Unearned privileges addressed
- Both have merit and limitations

- Central to political philosophy
- Shapes public policy debates
- Reflects different views on justice
- Responsibility and state role contested
