- Model demonstrates practical success

- Advocates for deeper democratic engagement
- Meaningful citizen involvement championed
- Robust and responsive democracy requires active citizenry
- Informed participation capability needed
- Direct contribution to democratic process
- Inspires democratic reforms worldwide
- Aims to bring politics closer to people

- Participatory model champions continuous citizen engagement

- Evidence from Porto Alegre and New England shows success

- True democracy requires cultivated, active citizenry
